About Us

A New Kind of Healthcare Company

HealthiQure Technologies Private Limited is an emerging Phygital healthcare startup founded in 2026, dedicated to transforming how healthcare is accessed and experienced across India — particularly in underserved and remote regions.

We operate at the intersection of healthcare, technology, and service integration, building a connected ecosystem that brings patients, doctors, hospitals, diagnostics, and emergency services into one seamless platform.

Phygital Model

Seamlessly blending physical presence with digital technology to deliver care where it's needed most.

Ecosystem Approach

Connecting patients, doctors, hospitals, diagnostics, and pharmacies into one unified platform.

Remote Region Focus

Deep focus on India's Northeast — bringing quality care to communities historically underserved by healthcare systems.

Technology-Driven

Leveraging digital tools to enable faster diagnosis, better doctor decision-making, and improved patient outcomes.

Our Philosophy

Every patient deserves the right care at the right time, without confusion or delay.
