Our Story

Healthcare Should Not Depend on Geography

ArogyaPath was founded in 2022 by a group of doctors and social workers from Arunachal Pradesh who witnessed firsthand how remote geography meant preventable deaths. Villages that took 6+ hours by road to reach a hospital. Mothers delivering without medical help. Children dying of treatable diseases.

We built ArogyaPath to bridge that gap using technology, affordable pricing, and the power of community health workers. Our mission: make quality healthcare available to every household in Arunachal Pradesh — free online and at ₹800 at your doorstep.
